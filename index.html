About us

What we do

We offer intensive training to all our riders to make them the safest and most professional riders on the streets. We train them on road safety, first aid, bike maintenance and customer care. We also make sure that they are always Equipped with hairnets and a spare helmet Identifiable, alarms connected to our security response team and always trackable through our system. When riders join/register to Guarded Boda community they see significant increase in their business providing impact to themselves, their families and the wider community. We came up with this idea to curb the pre-existing cases of robbery,assault etc. We have witnessed signicant changes in boda boda business which was our main goal.

How it works

  • Visit the website on your phone.


  • Register & login as a rider or Passeger.


  • Top up your Safiri Account using your Mpesa or bank .


  • State your destination and book for a ride.


  • The App will estimate the transportation payment which you will
    pay on the paybill number stated on website


  • You will be picked immediately after rider confirmation.

  • Enjoy the ride to your destination.
